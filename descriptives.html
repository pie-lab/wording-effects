Descriptive statistics

Last updated 2023-04-20

Participants (N = 974; 48.97% female) were, on average, 37.15 years old (SD = 14.51, minimum = 18, maximum = 84; see Figure 1A for the full distribution). A majority (66.74%) of participants identified as White only (10.37% only); Figure 1B shows the other response options and frequencies. See Figure 1C for the distribution of education, and 1D for the distribution of household income.
